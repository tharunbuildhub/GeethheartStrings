✉️ A Letter From My Heart ✨

Sometimes, I wonder if I'm even capable of loving 💖 someone again.

Not because I don't want to, but because I'm scared.

Scared of giving my whole heart 🫀 to someone and not receiving the kind of love ✨ I quietly dream about. Scared that one day the feelings might fade, or worse, that you might get tired of me. What if my personality becomes too much? What if my flaws become reasons for you to leave? These thoughts stay ⚓ with me every single day.

There are nights when I smile in front of everyone, only to come back to my room and silently fall apart. No one sees those moments. No one hears the conversations I have with myself, trying to convince my heart 🌙 that maybe one day, someone will love 💫 me the way I've always loved others.

I've spent so much of my life pretending I'm okay because I never wanted to become someone else's burden. But the truth is... sometimes I just want someone to notice that I'm not okay without me having to say a single word. 🍃

And yet...

Somehow, without even realizing it, I fell for you.

I don't know exactly when it happened. I don't know everything about you. But deep inside my heart, there's a strange peace that tells me you're someone I can trust. 🤝 I want to believe that you'll never betray my trust, never make me feel unwanted, and never choose someone else over me.

Because I've learned that the deepest wounds aren't always caused by strangers. Sometimes they're left by the people we trusted the most. That's why my heart hesitates before it believes. It isn't because it doubts you—it's because it's trying to protect the little pieces of itself that are still healing. 🛡️

I'm not looking for perfection.

I'm simply looking for a man who chosen 🌹 me every single day.

Someone who stands beside me proudly, supports my dreams, and never makes me feel like I'm asking for too much just because I need love. Someone who doesn't hesitate to hold my hand 🤝 through every season of life.

I hope ⭐ that even when you're angry, you never let harsh words hurt me. I'm a very sensitive person. The words spoken in anger stay in my heart much longer than anyone realizes. I don't expect you to be perfect, but I hope you'll protect my heart instead of breaking it.

Please never make me question where I stand in your life. Silence, distance, and mixed signals hurt me more than the truth ever could. Reassurance may seem like a small thing to you, but to someone who's afraid of being left behind, it means everything.

If you already have these qualities, I know I'll be the happiest girl in the world. ✨

If you don't, I hope you'll be willing to grow with me, just as I would for you. Because love isn't about finding someone perfect—it's about chosen each other enough to become better together.

The little things mean the most to me. 🌱

Ask me if I've eaten.

Ask me how my day was.

Wonder where I am because you care, not because you doubt me.

Celebrate my smallest moments.

If I send you pictures, I secretly hope your gallery becomes a collection of us, because every photo I send carries a piece of my happiness. 📸

Remember the little details I tell you—the things I like, the things that scare me, the stories I've only shared with you. Those little moments tell me I matter to you.

I don't need expensive gifts.

I don't need grand promises.

I only want to feel safe. 🏡

To feel chosen.

To feel loved without having to beg for it.

I want to be the person you run to, not the one you slowly drift away from.

I want to fall asleep knowing I was enough for someone—not because I was perfect, but because I was loved exactly as I am.

If one day I become quiet, please don't assume I've stopped caring. Sometimes my silence is just my heart asking, "Will you still chosen me if I stop trying so hard?"

Maybe that's all I've ever wanted—a love that stay, even on the hardest days. 🕊️

A love that doesn't make me question my worth.

A love where I never have to compete for attention, affection, or a place in someone's heart.

Because after everything life has taught me, I've realized that I don't need a perfect love story.

I just want a love that feels like home. 🏠

The kind of home where I can put down every fear I've carried for years, where I don't have to pretend to be strong all the time, and where my heart finally learns what peace feels like.

If I ever find that kind of love, I promise I'll protect it with everything I have. Because behind this smile is a girl who has survived more silent heartbreak than anyone knows... and all she's ever wanted was someone who would stay.

Forever yours
If you are reading this...
Thank you for listening to my heart.
❤️
May everyone find a love that stays.
